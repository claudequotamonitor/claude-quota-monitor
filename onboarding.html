Getting started — 3 steps

1

Open Claude.ai

Visit claude.ai and log in to your account. The extension captures your quota data automatically on page load — no setup required.

2

Check the toolbar badge

A badge will appear on the extension icon showing your current session usage (e.g. 22%). Color changes to orange at 70% and red at 90%.

3

Click for full details

Click the icon to see your session and weekly usage, remaining time until reset, and a manual refresh button. Data updates automatically every 10 minutes.

4

Pin the extension to your toolbar
